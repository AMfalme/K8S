Our Profile


The Stepping Stones Elementary School
Mission:
To train our students to become independent learners by equipping them with the necessary skills which will give them the ability to be good listeners, readers, speakers, researchers, innovators and problem solvers
Vision:
To inspire all our students to learn, grow, connect and thrive holistically as life-long learners and be a center of excellence.
Aims and Objectives:
  1. To clearly communicate to the students both orally and practically, information as prescribed in the curriculum and any other information that will impact knowledge.

  2. To stir the children to a point of becoming self-driven, and desiring success through hard work.
  3. To create a learning environment that promotes mastery of essential skills at every level.
  4. To connect with our community who are our partners in all aspects to ensure what we pass on to the students is upheld both in school and outside of school.
  5. To create a culture of nobility.

At the Stepping Stones Elementary school, we realize our pupil's futures rest in our hands. We provide them with a safe and secure environment that encourages them to realize their full potential. for us, it is vital to prepare our pupils to excel and flourish beyond Stepping Stones school. As a center of excellence, our standards are high and we strive to make learning exciting and holistic while ensuring that the pupils have the confidence to firmly grasp problems and develop solutions.
