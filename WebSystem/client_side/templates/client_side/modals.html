Principal Message

The Stepping Stones Elementary School is not just the usual setting designed for acquisition of literacy but an institution where self awareness is aroused and illuminated, refined and strengthened, a place where seeds of integrity and duty are sown and natured into fruition National goals and policies of education are objectively super but the means of achieving them is left solely to the schools. Implementation varies from school to school. We have innovated differentiated ways of imparting knowledge as well as transforming character in pursuit of holiness. Our staff members are the real beacons of light as they drive the main agenda of building on our vision and the core values. We have put in place infrastructure that is in line with the requirements prescribed by the developers of the new curriculum. Together with all the stakeholders we have and will always produce unique and unmatched products.
