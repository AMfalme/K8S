Aims and Objectives:
  1. To clearly communicate to the students both orally and practically, information as prescribed in the curriculum and any other information that will impact knowledge.

  2. To stir the children to a point of becoming self-driven, and desiring success through hard work.
  3. To create a learning environment that promotes mastery of essential skills at every level.
  4. To connect with our community who are our partners in all aspects to ensure what we pass on to the students is upheld both in school and outside of school.
  5. To create a culture of nobility.

At the Stepping Stones Elementary school, we realize our pupil's futures rest in our hands. We provide them with a safe and secure environment that encourages them to realize their full potential. for us, it is vital to prepare our pupils to excel and flourish beyond Stepping Stones school. As a center of excellence, our standards are high and we strive to make learning exciting and holistic while ensuring that the pupils have the confidence to firmly grasp problems and develop solutions. Principal Message

The Stepping Stones Elementary School is not just the usual setting designed for acquisition of literacy but an institution where self awareness is aroused and illuminated, refined and strengthened, a place where seeds of integrity and duty are sown and natured into fruition National goals and policies of education are objectively super but the means of achieving them is left solely to the schools. Implementation varies from school to school. We have innovated differentiated ways of imparting knowledge as well as transforming character in pursuit of holiness. Our staff members are the real beacons of light as they drive the main agenda of building on our vision and the core values. We have put in place infrastructure that is in line with the requirements prescribed by the developers of the new curriculum. Together with all the stakeholders we have and will always produce unique and unmatched products.
